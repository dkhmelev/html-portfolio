The best movies according to Khmelev

My top-3 movies of all the time

Forest Gump

A story of a retarded man with a big heart who doesn't care about his condition and overcomes obstacles through pain to the love.

Untouchables

Poor man starting to take care of a disabled millionaire. A beautiful picture of a true friendship born under difficulties that life brought to two extremely different fates.

Top Gear/Grand Tour

Not a full-length movie, but still cinematic as the best one. Three friends with extremely different understanding of what a car should be struggling in funny and challanging environments in a hope to understand whose car selection is the best.
